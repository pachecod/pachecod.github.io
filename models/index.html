3D Model Visualizations

Here are some 3D models I've managed to visualize using the A-frame 3D model viewer. From a desktop or laptop, you can use arrow keys or WASD keys to move around them. From a phone, you can look at them ina spherical way. I haven't yet figured out how to add navigation on phones, but am working on that.

  • Mars Perseverance Rover
    • Open in Safari on an iPhone and it will move with the accelerometer. It also works in a cardboard viewer when you click the VR button.
  • AME Zion Church
    • A-frame, for a GLB converted in Aero.
    • Google Model-Viewer method, for a GLB and USDZ.
  • Kissing Bench
