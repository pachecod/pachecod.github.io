Kissing Bench

The Kissing Bench is on the Syracuse University campus near the Hall of Languages and was a gift of the senior class memorial committee in 1912. A plaque in the center says that any couple who kisses on the bench will eventually marry.

Photogrammetry by Dan Pacheco using Reality Capture based on photos taken by Daniel Emerman.
