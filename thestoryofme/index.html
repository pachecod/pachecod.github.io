The Story of Dan Pacheco

Take a tour through all the places I've lived.

I was born in a town called Niceville in Florida, a place I don't remember because my parents moved almost immediately after I was born. My father was in the Air Force at the time and stationed at Eglin Air Force Base. For this reason, I really can't be considered a Floridian.

I turned 50 last week, and it occured to me that each decade of my life was dominated by a different places. Those are:

  • Era 1: 1971-early 1980s: Colorado (the Air Force Academy and Fort Collins)
    • Sub era: 1985-1986: The Hague, Holland
  • Era 2: 1986-1994: Colorado (Colorado Springs and Boulder)
  • Era 3: 1995-2004: The Washington, D.C. area.
  • Era 4: 2004-2012: Broomfield, Colorado
  • 2Era 5: 012-today: Syrause, NY

I thoroughy enjoyed each era, but do sometimes feel like I have lived like an electron circling an atom. On three separate occasions, I moved away from Colorado, but always returned. What will happen in my next era? Who knows? (But don't worry, I don't plan on leaving Syracuse any time soon.)
